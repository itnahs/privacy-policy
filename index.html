Privacy Policy for PlayO - Video & Music Player

Last updated: April 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how PlayO - Video & Music Player (“we”, “us”, “our”) handles information when you use the mobile application (package name: com.playo.player.media).

1. Summary

  • The app plays video and audio from your device and network sources you provide.
  • No account is required.
  • No analytics, ads, or crash-reporting services sending personal data to our servers.
  • Most data stays on your device.

2. Information the App accesses

2.1 Media on your device

The app may access video/audio files and metadata such as file name, path, duration, resolution, size, and modification time.

Permissions used:

  • READ_MEDIA_VIDEO
  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE / WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (older Android versions)

2.2 Network access

The app uses INTERNET permission when you open network streams or content URLs.

2.3 Playback and background operation

Foreground services may be used for uninterrupted playback.

2.4 Local data storage

  • App settings (DataStore)
  • Search history
  • Local media database (Room)

All this data remains on your device.

2.5 Sharing

Sharing uses Android system features. We do not control third-party apps you share content with.

2.6 Android backup

Backup behavior depends on your device and settings.

3. Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13.

4. Third-party services

The app uses open-source libraries (Jetpack Compose, Media3, Coil, etc.) only for functionality.

5. Security

We use reasonable measures, but no system is 100% secure.

6. Your rights

  • You can clear app data or uninstall the app.
  • You can revoke permissions in Android settings.

7. International users

Your data may be processed according to applicable laws.
